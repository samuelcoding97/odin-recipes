Dutch Babies

Delicious dutch babies right out of the oven

A perfectly delicious alternative to waffles or pancakes on a weekend morning!

Best served piping hot and with lemons and sugar

Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup whole milk, room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • pinch of nutmeg
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • fresh lemon, sugar and butter for topping
